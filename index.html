Gur Ka Rasgulla

Ingredients

  • Full-Cream Milk: 1000 ml (or as needed).
  • Jaggery (Gur): 1.5 cups (or to taste).
  • Corn Flour/Maida (Optional): 1 teaspoon (for chenna).
  • Cardamom Powder (Optional): 0.5 teaspoons

Recipe

  1. Boil the milk in a pan.
  2. Lower the flame and add lemon juice or vinegar. Stir gently until the milk curdles completely.
  3. Strain it using a muslin cloth. Rinse it with cold water to remove the sourness.
  4. Tie the cloth and hang it for 15-20 minutes to remove excess water. Making Rasgulla Balls: Take the chhena in a plate and knead it for 5-7 minutes until smooth. If the chhena is too dry, add 1 tsp water.
